Quad Props

HQProp HQ Prop 6x3.5

4 props in 1 purchase: * two props are labeled 6x3.5R, "pusher prop" "reverse". They are for clockwise (looking down at them), positions 3 and 4 (front left and rear right). Part num HQP010506351 * two props are labeled 6x3.5 (without the "R") "standard". They are for counterclockwise, positions 1 and 2 (front right and rear left). Part num HQP010506350

They will be mounted with the letters pointing up.

Hub width: 11mm
Hub thickness: 6mm
